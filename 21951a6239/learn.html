HR Management

What is HR Management?

HR management involves planning, organizing, and controlling the functions of recruiting, staffing, training, development, compensation, and employee relations to maintain a skilled and motivated workforce.

Why is HR Management Important?

Effective HR management ensures that organizations have the right talent in the right roles, fosters a positive work culture, promotes employee engagement and development, and ensures legal compliance.

Key Functions of HR Management

  • Recruitment and Selection
  • Training and Development
  • Performance Management
  • Compensation and Benefits
  • Employee Relations
  • HR Information Systems
  • Legal Compliance
