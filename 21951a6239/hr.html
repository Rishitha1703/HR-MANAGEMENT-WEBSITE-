HR New Hiring Process

Hiring Process Steps

  1. Applicant submits Resume with opportunity ID via email.
  2. Email content is extracted and stored.
  3. Opportunity ID is sent to respective HR (Finance, IM, etc.).
  4. HR verifies the resume; if shortlisted, forwards it to respective Managers.
  5. Manager schedules the interview & assigns a team member to perform the interview.
  6. Interviewer conducts the interview & updates the results.
  7. Manager updates based on the results.
  8. HR sends out the offer letter if selected.
  9. Applicant responds with Acceptance/Rejection subject line.
  10. On Rejection, the process ends. On Acceptance, a new employee ID/mail-id is generated, and the process ends.
